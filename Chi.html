Ағылшын тіліндегі CHINA тақырыбы (топик)

The People’s Republic of China is the third largest country in the world. It is located in Central and East Asia and borders with many countries, including Russia. From the east, China is washed by the waters of the Yellow, East China and South China seas of the Pacific Ocean.

The story of China is always accompanied by multi-digit numbers. This Asian country is unique in many ways. Its territory is 9.5 million. sq. km The population is 1 205 million. man, that is, 1/5 of the population of the globe.

The chronicle history of China goes back centuries for 3500 years. Naturally, the culture and customs of such a large and ancient country are very heterogeneous. Even people from different parts of China are not like each other. Except actually Chinese, in the country, live more than 50 nations, speaking different languages.

North, or Beijing, Chinese is the official language of China. It is spoken by the entire population, except for residents of the coastal southeastern regions, where Cantonese and other Chinese languages are spoken. Each of these languages has many dialects. But in the letter, they use hieroglyphs everywhere.

The nature of China is also varied. In the east and southeast, fertile plains stretch, in other regions mountains rise or deserts stretch. It’s not easy to arrange life in such a colossal multinational and multilingual country. From 1949, the Chinese authorities, following the USSR, tried to build socialism but achieved only the same clothes and poverty for all. Recently, however, China has managed to achieve significant economic success.

Since 1949, Chinese factories and plants have gradually been modernized, but compared to modern Japanese or American, of course, they have long been outdated. Nevertheless, China is the world's leading manufacturer of televisions and other electronics, agricultural machinery, tools and textiles. In a country with such a huge population, there is no shortage of workers.

Chinese culture is very ancient: the age of paintings of some ancient Chinese tombs exceeds 6000 years. Nowadays, traditional folk dances and music are alive. Movies, opera and theatre are very popular in China. The government encourages the creation of works of art about the successes of the Chinese people.
