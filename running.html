Ағылшын тіліндегі MY HOBBY IS RUNNING тақырыбы (топик)

One of my favourite hobbies is running. I really like running because first of all it's very healthy and useful for my body and mind. Doctors say that running and jogging help maintain a healthy heart and lungs. We also gain muscle strength. So if you go running several times a week, you will definitely stay in shape. You can do it anywhere: in a city park or in the country. Running is easy to start because it doesn’t require any special skills.

Besides, running is a cheap hobby. It doesn't cost you anything and you don’t need any equipment or gym membership. You should only invest money in a pair of trainers because good running shoes make it easier and less painful to run on solid surfaces, like pavement or concrete. Although I prefer to run along dirt trails or trodden paths.

I’ve been running since I was 15 years old. My friend often joins me. We don’t run long distances, it usually takes us about 20-30 minutes to run 3 or 4 kilometres three times a week. Stretching is also important after we run. It helps our muscles recover. To prevent my body from dehydration I always drink some water before and after running. Otherwise I may have bad headaches or dizziness.

My favorite time for running is in the morning, because it's nice and peaceful outside in the early hours. You can watch the sunrise, feel fresh air on your face, and of course you can hear birds singing. There are no crowds of people or noisy cars in the streets yet. Running in the morning helps me reduce stress.

After running I go home, have a shower, have my breakfast and go to the University. Running is fun! It’s my best medicine to keep healthy, happy and slim.
